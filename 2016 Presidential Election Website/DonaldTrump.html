Donald Trump's Early Life

  • Donald Trump was born on June 14, 1946 in Queens, New York
  • He was the fourth of five children in his family
  • When he turned 13 his parents sent him to the New York Military Academy
  • At the academy Trump was a star athlete and student leader when he graduated in 1964
  • Donald Trump attended Fordham University and the Wharton School of Finance at the University of Pennsylvania

Donald Trump in Reality TV

  • Trump produced and stared in a NBC reality series called the Apprentice.
  • In the Apprentice contestants competed for a management position within the Trump Organization.
  • The show was such a hit they created a spin of of the show called Celebrity Apprentice.
