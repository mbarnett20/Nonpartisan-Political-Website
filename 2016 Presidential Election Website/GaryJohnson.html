Gary Johnson's Early Life

  • Gary Johnson was born on Jan 1, 1953 in Minot, North Dakota.
  • He attended the University of New Mexico in 1975
  • In college he was a door-to-door handyman and that was such a success he started Big J Enterprises

Gary Johnson as Governor

  • Gary Johnson has had two terms as Governor of New Mexico.
  • He balanced the state's budget, and left New Mexico with a billion-dollar surplus.
  • He executed a major infrastructure overall and worked to improve schools

Other Facts

  • Gary Johnson is an avid skier, adventurer, ironman, and bicyclists and has scaled the highest peak on each of the seven continents, including Mt. Everest.
  • Gary Johnson has two grown children and lives in a house he built himself in Taos, New Mexico.
